Volunteer Listings

OnyxKing allows every volunteer to have A voice and give suggestions & other feedback, even if they are not apart of the writing department. For example, Generalisk from the 3D art department suggested animating viewers comments to celebrate 175k subs on youtube, this later became this video. OnyxKing also allows flexible work timings, you can work anytime you want as long as you meet deadlines, for example one hour one day and 12 the next. OnyxKing volunteers work remotely through Discord.

This listing is for the Story writer role for the OnyxKing web series.

Requirements:

  • Must have A strong understanding in modern day Meme Culture & be able to write episodes themed around them.
  • Must have A strong understanding of Nintendo and their IP's (mainly Mario).
  • Must have experience in story writing, mainly in the comedy genre.
  • Must be able to meet deadlines.
  • Have A great sense of humor.
  • Enjoy what we make (don't be A clout chaser).

Bonus (Not needed requirements but will help):

  • Come up with Original Character ideas.
  • Help write story arcs.
