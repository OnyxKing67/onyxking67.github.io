Full Name: Elena Dusk

Age: 16

Gender: Female

Pronouns: She/Her

Grasshopper was the first Original OnyxKing character, debuting in 2019 episode "Waluigi The Super Hero!!!". She had A Friday Night Funkin' mod in the works called VS Grasshopper, but is currently on A Indefinite Hiatus.

Bio

Born on December 15th, 2007, Elena Dusk grew up in the small town of Canvocer, located in the south-east side of the Mushroom kingdom, where she would attend the Cohnagher academy with her friends and her crush, Steven Smith. One day, she discovered A ancient architecture inside A cave while exploring the woods, which inside she discovers the ancient Grasshopper suit, forged with Tennanite by the ancestors that lived in the forest long ago. With it, she used its special powers to fight crime and help her friends.
