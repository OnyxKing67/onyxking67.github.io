Calem the Spider is A Original OnyxKing character, he debuted in 2021 in the episode "Spider Infestation".

Bio

Born into A cult that worshipped the almightly Wilskins coffee, Calem grew to reject their beliefs and fled his home in Brazil in search for A new home, which is where he ended up in the Mushroom kingdom (he actually tried to go to London, but he crashed the plane after he covered the pilots with cobwebs because they ran out of coffee), where surely the cult can't follow him there, right? ... right?
